Nature of Our Content

ShivaMarg is a devotional and educational resource. All mantras, stotras, shlokas, and scriptural content are presented in a spirit of reverence and are intended to support personal spiritual practice.

We do not claim scriptural authority. While we strive for accuracy in translations and transliterations, variations exist across different sampradayas (traditions), texts, and commentators. Readers are encouraged to consult a qualified guru, pandit, or traditional source for authoritative guidance.

Spiritual practices should be undertaken with proper guidance. Some mantras (particularly bija mantras and tantric stotras) have specific rules of recitation, initiation requirements, and contraindications. Please consult a qualified spiritual teacher before beginning any intensive mantra sadhana.
