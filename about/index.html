✦ Our Story ✦
What is ShivaMarg?

ShivaMarg (शिवमार्ग) — the path of Shiva, the path of auspiciousness — is a sacred digital space devoted to the eternal wisdom of Sanatan Dharma. We are a platform that brings hymns, mantras, stotras, aartis and scriptural verses of Hindu tradition to every seeker, with reverence and scholarly care.

Born from a personal journey of faith and a deep love for the Sanskrit literary tradition, ShivaMarg began as a small devotional project and has grown into a comprehensive resource for spiritual seekers, students of Vedic literature, and devout practitioners across the world.

Brihadaranyaka Upanishad — Our guiding prayer

May all be happy. May all be free from illness. May all see what is auspicious. May none suffer. This ancient Vedic prayer is the spirit behind everything we do at ShivaMarg.

✦ Mission & Vision ✦
Our Sacred Purpose

We believe that the wisdom of the Vedas, Upanishads, Puranas and the great devotional poets belongs to all humanity. Our mission is to make this wisdom accessible — clearly presented, beautifully designed, and deeply respectful of tradition.

📿
Authentic Texts
Every mantra and stotra is presented with care for authentic Sanskrit and proper Devanagari rendering.
🌍
Universal Access
Transliterations and Hindi meanings make these sacred texts accessible regardless of Sanskrit knowledge.
🪔
Living Tradition
Scriptures not as museum pieces, but as living texts — with context, practice guidance and spiritual significance.
🙏
Seva Bhava
This is a labour of devotion — not commerce. We are committed to keeping this resource free and accessible.
