About ShivMarg.live

ShivMarg.live is an online spiritual platform built to make daily devotional practice more accessible, organized, and personal. It brings together traditional religious texts with modern tools for tracking and reminders. The platform currently provides, or intends to provide:

  • Hindu mantras, chalisas, aartis, and stotras, presented for reading and recitation.
  • Spiritual articles covering festivals, practices, philosophy, and devotional guidance.
  • A Panchang (Hindu calendar) with astronomical and muhurat information.
  • A Jaap Tracker and Family Jaap Tracker for logging personal recitation practice.

  • Other devotional tools and religious festival information as the platform grows.

The website is intended for educational and devotional purposes. It is a companion to personal spiritual practice, not a replacement for the guidance of a qualified guru, priest, or religious institution.
