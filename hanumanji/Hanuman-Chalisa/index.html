श्री हनुमान चालीसा Shree Hanuman Chalisa

भगवान हनुमान जी को समर्पित संत तुलसीदास रचित श्री हनुमान चालीसा — हिंदी और अंग्रेज़ी में पूर्ण पाठ।

श्री हनुमान चालीसा (हिंदी में)

श्रीगुरु चरन सरोज रज निज मनु मुकुरु सुधारि।
बरनऊं रघुबर बिमल जसु जो दायकु फल चारि॥

बुद्धिहीन तनु जानिके सुमिरौं पवन कुमार।
बल बुद्धि बिद्या देहु मोहिं हरहु कलेस बिकार॥

जय हनुमान ज्ञान गुन सागर।
जय कपीस तिहुं लोक उजागर॥
रामदूत अतुलित बल धामा।
अंजनि पुत्र पवनसुत नामा॥

महाबीर बिक्रम बजरंगी।
कुमति निवार सुमति के संगी॥
कंचन बरन बिराज सुबेसा।
कानन कुंडल कुंचित केसा॥
हाथ बज्र औ ध्वजा बिराजै।
कांधे मूंज जनेऊ साजै॥
संकर सुवन केसरीनंदन।
तेज प्रताप महा जग बन्दन॥

विद्यावान गुनी अति चातुर।
राम काज करिबे को आतुर॥
प्रभु चरित्र सुनिबे को रसिया।
राम लखन सीता मन बसिया॥
सूक्ष्म रूप धरि सियहिं दिखावा।
बिकट रूप धरि लंक जरावा॥
भीम रूप धरि असुर संहारे।
रामचंद्र के काज संवारे॥
लाय सजीवन लखन जियाये।
श्रीरघुबीर हरषि उर लाये॥
रघुपति कीन्ही बहुत बड़ाई।
तुम मम प्रिय भरतहि सम भाई॥
सहस बदन तुम्हरो जस गावैं।
अस कहि श्रीपति कंठ लगावैं॥
सनकादिक ब्रह्मादि मुनीसा।
नारद सारद सहित अहीसा॥

जम कुबेर दिगपाल जहां ते।
कबि कोबिद कहि सके कहां ते॥
तुम उपकार सुग्रीवहिं कीन्हा।
राम मिलाय राज पद दीन्हा॥
तुम्हरो मंत्र बिभीषन माना।
लंकेस्वर भए सब जग जाना॥
जुग सहस्र जोजन पर भानू।
लील्यो ताहि मधुर फल जानू॥
प्रभु मुद्रिका मेलि मुख माहीं।
जलधि लांघि गये अचरज नाहीं॥

दुर्गम काज जगत के जेते।
सुगम अनुग्रह तुम्हरे तेते॥
राम दुआरे तुम रखवारे।
होत न आज्ञा बिनु पैसारे॥
सब सुख लहै तुम्हारी सरना।
तुम रक्षक काहू को डर ना॥
आपन तेज सम्हारो आपै।
तीनों लोक हांक तें कांपै॥
भूत पिसाच निकट नहिं आवै।
महाबीर जब नाम सुनावै॥
नासै रोग हरै सब पीरा।
जपत निरंतर हनुमत बीरा॥
संकट तें हनुमान छुड़ावै।
मन क्रम बचन ध्यान जो लावै॥
सब पर राम तपस्वी राजा।
तिन के काज सकल तुम साजा॥
और मनोरथ जो कोई लावै।
सोइ अमित जीवन फल पावै॥
चारों जुग परताप तुम्हारा।
है परसिद्ध जगत उजियारा॥
साधु संत के तुम रखवारे।
असुर निकंदन राम दुलारे॥

अष्ट सिद्धि नौ निधि के दाता।
अस बर दीन जानकी माता॥
राम रसायन तुम्हरे पासा।
सदा रहो रघुपति के दासा॥
तुम्हरे भजन राम को पावै।
जनम-जनम के दुख बिसरावै॥
अन्तकाल रघुबर पुर जाई।
जहां जन्म हरि भक्त कहाई॥
और देवता चित्त न धरई।
हनुमत सेइ सर्ब सुख करई॥
संकट कटै मिटै सब पीरा।
जो सुमिरै हनुमत बलबीरा॥
जै जै जै हनुमान गोसाईं।
कृपा करहु गुरुदेव की नाईं॥
जो सत बार पाठ कर कोई।
छूटहि बंदि महा सुख होई॥
जो यह पढ़ै हनुमान चालीसा।
होय सिद्धि साखी गौरीसा॥
तुलसीदास सदा हरि चेरा।
कीजै नाथ हृदय मंह डेरा॥

पवन तनय संकट हरन मंगल मूरति रूप।
राम लखन सीता सहित हृदय बसहु सुर भूप॥

Having cleansed the mirror of my mind with the dust of my Guru's lotus feet, I describe the pure glory of Shri Ram, the giver of the four fruits of life.

Knowing my body devoid of wisdom, I remember the Son of the Wind. Grant me strength, wisdom and knowledge, and remove my sorrows and flaws.

Victory to Hanuman, ocean of wisdom and virtue. Victory to the King of monkeys, who illuminates the three worlds. Messenger of Ram, abode of matchless strength, known as the son of Anjani and the Wind.

Great hero, mighty as a thunderbolt, remover of bad thoughts and companion of good sense. Golden-hued, well adorned, with curly hair and earrings. Holding a mace and a flag, wearing a sacred thread on the shoulder. Son of Shiva, delight of Kesari, of great glory, worshipped throughout the world.

Learned, virtuous and highly skilled, always eager to perform Ram's work. Devoted to hearing Ram's stories, with Ram, Lakshman and Sita dwelling in your heart. You appeared in a tiny form before Sita, and in a giant form you burnt Lanka. Taking a fearsome form you destroyed demons, and accomplished Ram's tasks. You brought the Sanjeevani herb and revived Lakshman, and Ram embraced you joyfully. Ram praised you greatly, calling you as dear as his brother Bharat. Thousand-faced Sheshnag sings your glory, so said Ram embracing you close. Sanak and other sages, Brahma and other great seers, Narad, Saraswati and Sheshnag too.

Yama, Kubera, the guardians of directions, poets and scholars — none can fully describe your glory. You helped Sugriv and united him with Ram, granting him his kingdom. Vibhishan accepted your counsel and became the king of Lanka, as the whole world knows. The sun, thousands of yojanas away, you swallowed thinking it a sweet fruit. Carrying Ram's ring in your mouth, you crossed the ocean; no wonder at all.

Whatever difficult task exists in this world becomes easy by your grace. You are the guardian at Ram's door; none enter without your permission. All happiness is found under your shelter; with you as protector, there is nothing to fear. You alone can withstand your own splendour; the three worlds tremble at your roar. Ghosts and evil spirits cannot come near when your mighty name is uttered. All ailments and pains vanish for one who constantly chants your name, O brave Hanuman. Hanuman rescues from every trouble one who meditates on you in thought, word and deed. Ram, the great ascetic king, above all, and you accomplish all his tasks. Whoever brings any wish to you receives the limitless fruit of life. Your glory shines in all four ages, renowned and radiant across the world. You are the protector of saints and sages, and the beloved of Ram, destroyer of demons.

Giver of the eight siddhis and nine treasures, this boon was granted by Mother Janaki (Sita). You hold the elixir of devotion to Ram, and remain forever the servant of Raghupati. Through devotion to you, one attains Ram, and sorrows of many lifetimes are forgotten. At the end of life one goes to Ram's abode, and is known there as Hari's devotee. One who keeps no other deity in the heart, and serves Hanuman, attains all happiness. All troubles and pains are destroyed for one who remembers brave Hanuman. Victory, victory, victory to Hanuman, Lord; bestow your grace as a true Guru would. Whoever recites this a hundred times is freed from bondage and attains great joy. Whoever reads this Hanuman Chalisa attains success, with Shiva as the witness. Tulsidas, forever a servant of Hari, prays: O Lord, make your home in my heart.

O Son of the Wind, remover of troubles, embodiment of auspiciousness, dwell in my heart along with Ram, Lakshman and Sita, O King of the gods.

Shree Hanuman Chalisa: About the Text

Who wrote Hanuman Chalisa?

श्री हनुमान चालीसा की रचना 16वीं शताब्दी में संत कवि गोस्वामी तुलसीदास जी ने अवधी भाषा में की थी। यह चालीसा भगवान हनुमान जी की महिमा, बल, भक्ति और गुणों का वर्णन करती है और आज भी करोड़ों भक्तों द्वारा प्रतिदिन पढ़ी जाती है।

Hanuman Chalisa was composed by the poet-saint Goswami Tulsidas, best known for writing the Ramcharitmanas. Written in the Awadhi language, it consists of 40 chaupais framed by opening and closing dohas, describing Hanuman ji's strength, devotion and virtues.

How can anyone read Hanuman Chalisa?

स्नान करके स्वच्छ स्थान पर हनुमान जी की मूर्ति या चित्र के सामने बैठें, दीपक या अगरबत्ती जलाएं, हाथ जोड़कर शांत मन से चालीसा का पाठ करें। इसे एक बार, सात बार या 108 बार पढ़ा जा सकता है, विशेषकर मंगलवार और शनिवार को।

Sit facing an image or idol of Hanuman ji, ideally after bathing, in a clean and quiet space. Light a diya or incense, fold your hands, and recite the chalisa slowly, focusing on its meaning. It may be read once, or traditionally 7 or 108 times, especially on Tuesdays and Saturdays.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who wrote Hanuman Chalisa?

Hanuman Chalisa was composed by Goswami Tulsidas in the 16th century in the Awadhi language.

How many chaupais are there in Hanuman Chalisa?

It contains 40 chaupais along with two opening dohas and one closing doha.

How can anyone read Hanuman Chalisa?

Sit calmly facing Hanuman ji's image after bathing, light a diya, and recite slowly with focus on the meaning. It can be read once daily, or 7 or 108 times on Tuesdays and Saturdays.

What is the best time to read Hanuman Chalisa?

Early morning after a bath or evening before sleeping are considered best, with Tuesdays and Saturdays being especially auspicious for Hanuman worship.
