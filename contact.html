✦ Contribute ✦
Help Grow ShivaMarg

ShivaMarg grows through the collective effort of devoted contributors. If you have expertise in Sanskrit, Vedic texts, Hindi translation, or know of a stotra that should be included — please reach out. Your seva will be credited and deeply appreciated.

We are especially looking for contributions in: Vedic hymns (Rigveda, Samaveda), regional devotional songs, regional language translations (Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Marathi, Bengali), and audio recordings of traditional recitations.
